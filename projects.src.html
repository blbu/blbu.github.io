Armies at War

Originally a text-based browser army-building game (2005-2011), Armies at War is my forever-project. It has been a PHP/MySQL project, a Python/SQLite project, and most recently, and Android app.

The latest version, an unfinished Android app, is built on a Flask back-end, which I highly recommend for micro-APIs.
