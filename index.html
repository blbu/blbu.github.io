"Software Developer"

People have problems they want to solve. Sometimes, using computers makes solving that problem easier, or makes an otherwise impossible problem solvable.

A Software Developer, or "programmer" takes a problem and creates a program that solves that problem.

Some kinds of problems require more thinking before they can be solved, and sometimes other people can apply their skills to turn the original problem into smaller sub-problems that are more easily solved from a developer's perspective. "Analysis"

Currently, almost everything I do requres analysis.
